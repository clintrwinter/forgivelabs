Built on decades of research

The science behind the companion.

We draw from the full body of forgiveness research — not one model, but many. Because no two people carry the same wound the same way, and no single framework reaches everyone.

E
Emotional Forgiveness
Replacing the negative emotions of resentment and bitterness with positive, other-oriented feelings. Not deciding to forgive — feeling it. This is where lasting freedom lives.
S
Shame Detox
Research shows shame — "I am bad" — does more damage than guilt — "I did something bad." Separating what happened to you from who you are is the core of identity restoration.
C
Compassionate Accountability
Genuine self-forgiveness requires holding two things at once: real responsibility for what happened, and real compassion for yourself as a fallible human being. Neither cancels the other.
R
Rumination Release
Every time you replay the wound, you strengthen the neural pathway that keeps it alive. Learning to interrupt and redirect that cycle — without suppressing it — is a clinical skill we build together.
I
Identity Reclamation
Sustained hurt rewires how you see yourself. The work isn't just forgiving the other person — it's recovering the self that the wound distorted. Who are you without this weight?
P
Paced Readiness
Forgiveness cannot be forced or rushed. Research shows that interventions paced to individual readiness produce significantly better outcomes. Your timeline is the only right one.

01

Raw grief, quiet numbness, cautious curiosity — all of it is the right starting point. No preparation, no right words, no right emotional state required.
02
Choose your lens
Before the conversation begins, you select a conversational persona — a lens through which the companion will engage with you. Whether you need a steady witness, a gentle mirror, or a forward-looking guide, you are always in control of the tone and depth.

The companion guides you through a living, adaptive conversation drawn from decades of forgiveness research. Not a rigid script — a sequence that bends to your readiness. Pause, hold a thread, or slow down at any moment.
